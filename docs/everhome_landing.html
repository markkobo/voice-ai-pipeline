Ethical guardrails

The cliff is real. We've thought about it.

Voice cloning a deceased relative is powerful and easily misused. Four non-negotiable guardrails ship before any commercial release.

  • Consent capture The recorded person must opt in on camera before training begins. No exceptions.
  • Disclosure rule The AI reveals it is AI if directly asked. The system prompt enforces this; the model is trained around it.
  • Audit trail Every training event logged. Every inference logged. Exportable on demand to the consent holder.
  • Watermarking Generated audio carries an inaudible mark so a clone cannot be passed off as the real person in evidentiary or legal contexts.
